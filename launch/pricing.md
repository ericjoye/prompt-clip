# Prompt Clip — Pricing Strategy

## Recommended Model: Freemium (Free + One-Time Pro Upgrade)

### Free Tier — $0 (forever)
- 5 built-in prompt templates (Summarize, Explain, Translate, Find Action Items, Code Review)
- Auto-context framing (page title, URL, date)
- Smart code/prose detection
- Keyboard shortcut
- Options page with template toggles
- All core functionality

### Pro Tier — $4.99 one-time purchase
- Everything in Free, plus:
- Unlimited custom templates (create your own prompts)
- Template categories and folders
- Export prompt history to Markdown/JSON
- Custom keyboard shortcuts per template
- Priority support via email
- Early access to new templates

## Rationale

**Why one-time over subscription?**
- Chrome extension users strongly prefer one-time purchases over subscriptions for utility tools
- $4.99 is the sweet spot: low enough to impulse-buy, high enough to fund development
- Competitors (Copy as Markdown for AI) use free-only or subscription — one-time is a differentiator
- Reduces churn anxiety and support burden

**Why not free-only?**
- Custom templates are the #1 requested feature and justify a paid tier
- Revenue funds continued development and Chrome Web Store fees
- Even 2-5% conversion on a free tool with good distribution generates meaningful income

## Alternative: Donation-Supported
If Pro tier adds too much complexity for v1.0, ship entirely free with a "Support this project" link (GitHub Sponsors / Buy Me a Coffee). This maximizes adoption and reviews, with revenue as a bonus.

## Recommendation
Ship v1.0 as **free-only** to maximize adoption and reviews. Add Pro tier in v1.1 after gathering user feedback and feature requests. This follows the "ship fast, monetize later" approach from the portfolio strategy.
